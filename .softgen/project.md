# K A K ELECTRICALS — Landing Page

## Vision
Professional landing page for K A K ELECTRICALS, a highly-rated electrical services business in Craigieburn VIC. Converts visitors into customers through clear service presentation, social proof, and easy contact access. Target audience: homeowners and businesses needing electrical work or emergency repairs.

## Design
Color system (HSL format):
- `--primary: 220 90% 40%` (professional blue — trust, expertise)
- `--accent: 45 95% 50%` (electric gold — energy, brightness)
- `--background: 220 10% 97%` (clean light gray)
- `--foreground: 220 15% 20%` (dark slate text)
- `--muted: 220 10% 90%` (subtle backgrounds)

Typography:
- Headings: Outfit (weights 400, 600, 700) — modern, confident
- Body: Inter (weights 400, 500, 600) — clean, professional

Style: Clean, professional, trust-focused. High contrast, clear CTAs, mobile-first responsive design.

## Features
Single-page landing site with:
- Hero section with contact form
- Services grid (9 electrical services)
- Why Us section (trust indicators)
- Gallery showcase
- FAQ accordion
- Final CTA section
- Fixed header with phone number
- Responsive mobile design
